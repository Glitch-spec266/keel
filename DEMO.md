# Keel — CAC demo video script (~2 min)

## 1. Hook (problem) — 15s
> "5.4 million U.S. students secretly care for a sick family member. Every caregiver app is built for adults. **None are built for them.**"

Cite on screen: Johns Hopkins 2024 youth-caregiver study · U.S. GAO 2025 report directing HHS to clarify support for under-18 caregivers.

## 2. Today screen — 15s
Sign in as the teen. Show the calm glass home card surfacing the next care task and today's progress ring. One glance = what's next. (Screenshot: `docs/screenshots/03-today.png`)

## 3. Pillar 1: schedule → reminder → impact — 30s
1. Tap **+** → add "Grandma's 6pm medication", dose "1 pill", repeats every day, remind 10 min before.
2. Show the local notification firing ("Keel reminder — Grandma's 6pm medication").
3. Check it off → log "15 minutes" → open **My impact**: hours tick up, streak appears, and note the **Export my hours** button ("useful if your school offers service credit").

## 4. Pillar 2: verification → consent → resources — 35s
1. **Support** tab → "Request verification" from Sample High School → status *Pending*.
2. Switch to the counselor account → **Requests** inbox → **Verify**.
3. Show **Students** is still *empty* — "privacy first: no consent, no visibility."
4. Back as the teen → flip **"Share my care schedule"** consent ON → the **Resources** feed unlocks: respite, food boxes, mental-health circle, legal, financial, training — filterable by category.
5. Back as the counselor → the student now appears with care load at a glance; push a resource to them.

## 5. Privacy beat — 15s
Open **Profile → "Who can see my data"** → one org listed → tap **Revoke** → gone.
> "Row-level security, explicit consent, anonymous handles, biometric app lock — designed for minors handling health information."

## 6. Close — 10s
Show the same app on a Windows desktop browser and on an iPhone (SideStore).
> "**Keel — so no student has to carry it alone.**"
